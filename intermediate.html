Visit National Geographic Kids

National Geographic Kids is a website and magazine all about exploring the world around us. On this site, you'll find lots of cool articles, pictures, videos, and games about animals, planets, ancient civilizations, and other fascinating topics.

New York Times Learning Network

The New York Times Learning Network is a website created by The New York Times newspaper to help students learn about current events and develop critical thinking skills. The goal is to use the newspaper's content to help students become better readers, writers, and thinkers.

Visit BBC Newsround

BBC Newsround is a news website and TV program made specifically for children and young people. It covers news stories from around the world in a way that is easy for kids to understand. BBC Newsround helps kids stay informed about what's going on in the world.

Visit PBS LearningMedia

PBS LearningMedia is a website with lots of videos, games, and activities for kids and students. It's made by PBS, which is a television channel that makes educational shows. On this website, you can find fun stuff to help you learn about all kinds of topics like science, math, history, and more.

Visit Newsela

Newsela is a website that provides news articles and stories written at different reading levels for students. The same news story is available in multiple versions, from an easier reading level with simpler vocabulary up to a more advanced level with more complex language.

Visit TIME for Kids

Time for Kids is a magazine made for children. It has articles about news, science, and fun topics like sports and animals. The magazine helps kids learn about the world in a way that is easy to understand. It also includes cool pictures and fun activities.
