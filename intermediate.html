Visit Newswise

Newswise is a website where scientists, universities, and other groups share their new discoveries and important news. The news stories on Newswise are written in a way that is easy for people to understand.

New York Times Learning Network

The New York Times Learning Network is a website created by The New York Times newspaper to help students learn about current events and develop critical thinking skills.

Visit BBC Newsround

BBC Newsround is a news website and TV program made specifically for children and young people. It covers news stories from around the world in a way that is easy for kids to understand.

visit Scholastic News

Scholastic News is a classroom magazine and website that brings current events and news to students in an easy-to-understand way. The stories are written specifically for kids, using simple language and engaging visuals.

Visit Newsela

Newsela is a website that provides news articles and stories written at different reading levels for students. The same news story is available in multiple versions, from an easier reading level with simpler vocabulary up to a more advanced level with more complex language.

Visit TIME for Kids

Time for Kids is a magazine made for children. It has articles about news, science, and fun topics like sports and animals. The magazine helps kids learn about the world in a way that is easy to understand. It also includes cool pictures and fun activities.
