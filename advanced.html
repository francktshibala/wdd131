Visit News Decoder

News Decoder is a global educational news service aimed at helping young people develop a deeper understanding of world issues through journalism. It partners with schools to offer programs that teach students how to research, write, and think critically about global events. Students can participate in webinars, storytelling contests, and journalism projects,

Visit Upfront Magazine

Upfront Magazine, provided by Scholastic, is designed to engage students with current events and significant historical topics. It's a resource used by teachers to help students improve their reading, critical thinking, and media literacy skills. The magazine covers a range of subjects, including U.S. and world news, history, science, and culture.

Visit Teaching Kids News

Teaching Kids News is a website that provides current news articles written specifically for children. The site includes features to help kids think critically about the news. Each article comes with discussion prompts, grammar tips, and reading questions that align with school curriculums. All articles are written in a way that's easy for kids to understand. They cover a variety of topics including politics, science, sports, and entertainment.

Visit HuffPost Teen

HuffPost Teen is a section of the Huffington Post that targets teenagers. It offers a wide range of content including news, advice on navigating high school and college life, and stories on various topics that interest young readers. The content is designed to be engaging and relevant, often featuring articles written by or about teenagers. The section aims to inform and inspire young readers by covering important social issues, pop culture, and personal development topics.
